About

Established in 2001, SVCE - Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering is a premier Institute located in Karnataka. A leading institute in the city Degree courses including 10 UG and 6 PG courses. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. SVCE - Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering is a premier institute for B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, MBA/PGDM courses. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like B.E. / B.Tech, M.E./M.Tech, MBA/PGDM with the stream of Engineering, Business & Management Studies, IT & Software. Institute give offer to students to gain proficiency & expertise in the specialization of Mechatronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering. Courses offered by institute are recognized and acknowlegded by approving bodies like AICTE. Most importantly, these courses with 954 seats are imparted at affordable fees, which increase accessibility and allow students the opportunity to gain knowledge and skills in their chosen field. Courses offered by the Institute are in the fee range of INR 116,940-360,240 for a total of 954 candidates. SVCE - Sri Venkateshwara College of Engineering offers good infrastructure like Boys Hostel, Cafeteria, Girls Hostel, Hostel, Labs, Library, Shuttle Service, Sports Complex, Wi-Fi Campus to students.
